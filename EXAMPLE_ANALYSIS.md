## How to Interpret the Dashboard

### Color Coding
ðŸŸ¢ **Green** = Good/Safe (Buy signals, low risk)
ðŸŸ¡ **Yellow** = Moderate (Medium risk, average opportunity)
ðŸ”´ **Red** = Risky (High volatility, more dangerous)

### Score Interpretation
- **70-100**: Excellent opportunities - execute soon
- **50-70**: Good opportunities - solid choices
- **30-50**: Okay opportunities - risky/niche
- **0-30**: Skip these - wait for better

### Confidence Interpretation
- **80-100%**: Very likely to profit - go for it!
- **60-80%**: Probably profitable - reasonable choice
- **40-60%**: Risky - needs market knowledge
- **0-40%**: Very risky - avoid unless experienced

### ROI Interpretation
- **>20%**: Exceptional (rare, high risk usually)
- **10-20%**: Excellent (good target)
- **5-10%**: Good (solid, safe)
- **2-5%**: Acceptable (high volume helps)
- **<2%**: Skip it - not worth the time

   - Look for green ðŸŸ¢ low-risk items

2. **Buy Phase** (10 min)
   - Go to Grand Exchange
   - Search for items in your filter
   - Buy at recommended prices
   - Wait for transactions to complete

3. **During Day** (5 min)
   - Check if prices are rising
   - Monitor your items in bank

4. **Sell Phase** (10 min)
   - Come back after hold time

5. **Reinvest**
